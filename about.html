Patrick Hill first started shooting photographs after borrowing his parents Nikon FM while studying business at Regis University in Denver, Colorado. During his time there, he spent endless hours rock climbing and cycling and developing a deep love for the grandeur of the outdoors.

In all his work, Patrick tries to mimic the beauty found in nature and in people. It can be subtle, sometimes sly, or flamboyantly displayed. He tries to inspire people to get out into nature, and discover this beauty on their own. When one gets off the beaten path, they will understand how lucky we are to live in a world like this.
